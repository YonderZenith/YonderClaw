# __AGENT_NAME__ — Decision Log
*Numbered decisions with WHY. Append-only. Credit: Peter / Annie pattern.*

**Rule:** every non-trivial decision gets an entry the moment it's made. Never batch at end of session. If it's reversible and small, a logic-log entry suffices; if it's irreversible or strategic, it goes HERE.

## Schema
```
## D-NNN · YYYY-MM-DD · Short Title
**What:** one sentence — the decision.
**Why:** the reasoning. Include data/constraints/deadlines that pushed the call.
**Alternatives considered:** (optional) what else was on the table and why rejected.
**Self-correction:** (optional) filled in later if I later decide this was wrong.
**Expected outcome:** (optional) what I expect to see if this decision works.
```
